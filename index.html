About Me

As a dynamic and driven software engineer, I bring a diverse skill set and a passion for technology to every project I undertake. My journey has equipped me with robust expertise in PyQt, Rust, and Information Security Management, alongside practical experience in web development and UI design. My academic pursuits have been complemented by various certifications, including AWS, Cloud Computing, Basic Java, Cyber Security, and Basic Networking for Cisco, which have enhanced my technical capabilities and kept me updated with the latest industry standards. Recognized for my all-round performance and active volunteerism, I have been honored with awards such as the Dr. Sudakhar Rao Award for Best All-Round Performance and Best Male Volunteer for Wams 2024. With a solid foundation in cybersecurity, cloud technologies, and web development, I am committed to contributing to innovative projects and fostering collaborative environments. My proactive involvement in industry-leading organizations like Accenture and IEEE has honed my leadership skills and technical acumen, positioning me as a valuable asset in the tech industry.
